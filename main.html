UKRAINIAN TRADE LINKS

Our company

Ukrainian Trade Links LLC is a young and ambitious Ukrainian trading company. Our main objective is to establish strong trade relations between producers and customers in Ukraine and the countries of European Union. We do not only buy and sell, but we get in touch with the suppliers, visit production facilities and carry out technological analysis of the production. Our main aim here is to focus production on specific customer needs. In this way we match supply and demand in the European Union and Ukraine. We are always opened for dialog. Currently we already cooperate with 80 producer of solid biofuel in Ukraine, 4 mining companies and a number of processing plants of Ukraine and other foreign countries (Poland, Czech Republic, Italy, Russia, etc.) Despite the fact that our main office is in the capital of Ukraine – Kiev, our managers are always moving through the country, looking for new business partners and ways to improve logistics aspects.
